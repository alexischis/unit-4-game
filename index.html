Crystals Collector

You will be given a random value at the beginning of the game!

There a 4 crystals below; by clicking on a crystal you will add a specific number of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random value given at the beginning of the game. You lose the game if your total score goes over the random value.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it; these values reset at the start of a new game.
